DriveMatrix — Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use & protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all users.

  • Personal Details: Name, phone number, email, address.
  • Booking Data: Vehicle details, rental dates, location.
  • Payment Information: Only processed through secure payment gateways.
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser, IP, device type, location permissions (optional).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only for legitimate service needs.

  • To complete bookings and process payments.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To verify identity and prevent fraud.
  • To improve app/website performance.
  • To send booking notifications & updates.

3. Data Sharing Policy

We do not sell your personal information.

  • Payment Partners: For secure payment processing.
  • Legal Authorities: Only if required by law.
  • Service Providers: For drivers or delivery operations.

4. Data Protection & Security

  • Data is encrypted during storage and transfer.
  • We use industry-grade security systems.
  • Only authorized personnel can access sensitive data.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve user experience.

  • To save login sessions.
  • To improve website loading speed.
  • To analyze user traffic and performance.

6. User Rights

You can request the following at any time:

  • Access the personal data we hold.
  • Request deletion of your account.
  • Stop receiving emails / SMS notifications.
  • Edit or update your information.

7. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 16 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Users will be notified for major changes.
